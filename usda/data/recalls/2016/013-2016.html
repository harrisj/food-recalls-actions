Marin Pasta Works Recalls Pork Products Produced Without Benefit of Inspection

WASHINGTON, Feb. 5, 2016 – Marin Pasta Works, a San Rafael, Calif. establishment, is recalling approximately 491 pounds of pork ravioli products that were produced without the benefit of federal inspection, the U.S. Department of Agriculture’s Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS) announced today.
