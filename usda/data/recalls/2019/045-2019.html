Yoakum Packing Co. Recalls Venison Sausage Products Due to Mislabeling

Yoakum Packing Co.
Active
Wed, 04/10/2019 - Current
Texas

WASHINGTON, April 10, 2019 Yoakum Packing Co., a Yoakum, Texas establishment, is recalling approximately 12,388 pounds of smoked venison sausage that contains pork products that were not represented on the label, the U.S. Department of Agricultures Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS) announced today. The...

Impacted Products
2.5-lb. plastic wrapped bags containing frozen sausage links of “VENISON Smoked Sausage – FULLY COOKED – KEEP REFRIGERATED” and case code 35710 or 35712 represented on the label.
