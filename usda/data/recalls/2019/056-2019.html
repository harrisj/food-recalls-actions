BEF Foods, Inc. Recalls Beef Products Produced without Benefit of Inspection

BEF Foods, Inc.
Active
Tue, 05/21/2019 - Current
Alabama, Georgia, Illinois, Indiana, Kentucky, Michigan, Ohio

WASHINGTON, May 21, 2019 BEF Foods, Inc., a Lima, Ohio establishment, is recalling approximately 527 pounds of beef pasta products that were produced without the benefit of federal inspection and outside of inspection hours, the U.S. Department of Agricultures Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS)...

Impacted Products
18-oz. sealed plastic trays containing “Bob Evans Beef Stroganoff Pasta” with a USE BY date of 07/02/19 and a time-stamp ranging between the hours of 15:00 and 18:37 represented on the tray seal.
