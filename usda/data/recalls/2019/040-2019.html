AdvancePierre Foods, Inc. Recalls Frozen Beef Patties due to Possible Foreign Matter Contamination

AdvancePierre Foods, Inc.
Closed
Tue, 04/02/2019 - Fri, 05/31/2019

WASHINGTON, April 2, 2019 AdvancePierre Foods, Inc., an Enid, Okla. establishment, is recalling approximately 20,373 pounds of ready-to-eat (RTE) beef patties that may be contaminated with extraneous materials, specifically soft purple plastic, the U.S. Department of Agricultures Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS) announced today...

Impacted Products
14.06-lb. cases containing three bags with 30 pieces for a total of 90 portions of “CN FULLY COOKED FLAMEBROILED BEEF PATTIES CARAMEL COLOR ADDED” with case code 155-525-0 and package code 8334.
Quantity Recovered
9,898 pounds
