Fulton Seafood Inc. Recalls Siluriformes Products Produced Without Benefit of Inspection

Closed
Fri, 02/22/2019 - Fri, 02/14/2020
Texas

WASHINGTON, Feb. 22, 2019 Fulton Seafood Inc., a Houston, Texas establishment, is recalling approximately 100,924 pounds of Siluriformes fish products, specifically wild caught catfish, because the products were produced, packed, and distributed without the benefit of federal inspection, the U.S. Department of Agricultures Food Safety...

Impacted Products
60-lb. brown cardboard boxes containing “River Catfish Wild USA.” Upon arrival at retail locations, the catfish were removed from boxes and placed in the seafood display case for consumer purchase.
Quantity Recovered
20,647 pounds
