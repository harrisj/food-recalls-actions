Oklahoma Firm Recalls Chicken Fried Chicken Breasts That May Contain Foreign Materials

Closed
Fri, 01/25/2013 - Tue, 03/24/2015

WASHINGTON, Jan 25, 2013 -Advance Pierre Foods, an Enid, Okla. establishment, is recalling approximately 1,200 pounds of chicken fried chicken breasts that may contain small pieces of metal, the U.S. Department of Agriculture's Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS) announced today.
