Kansas Firm Recalls Ground Beef Products Due To Possible E. coli O157:H7 Contamination

National Beef Packing Co., L.L.C.
Closed
Wed, 07/31/2013 - Tue, 03/24/2015

WASHINGTON, July 31, 2013 National Beef Packing Company, a Liberal, Kan., firm, is recalling approximately 50,100 pounds of ground beef products that may be contaminated with E. coli O157:H7, the U.S. Department of Agriculture's Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS) announced today.
