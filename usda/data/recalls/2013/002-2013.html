Illinois Firm Recalls Ready-To-Eat and Raw Poultry Products Produced Without Benefit of Inspection

Humphrey's Market, Inc.
Closed
Thu, 01/17/2013 - Tue, 03/24/2015
Kansas, Missouri, Nebraska

WASHINGTON, Jan 17, 2013 -Humphrey's Market, Inc., a Springfield, Ill. establishment, is recalling approximately 2,764 pounds of frozen, ready-to-eat (RTE) and raw poultry products because they were produced without the benefit of inspection. The products were also misbranded after being repackaged and may contain soy...
