California Firm Recalls Ground Beef Products That May Contain Foreign Materials

WASHINGTON, Sept. 19, 2013 Central Valley Meat Company, a Hanford, Calif., establishment, is recalling 58,240 pounds of ground beef that may contain small pieces of plastic, the U.S. Department of Agriculture's Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS) announced today. The products subject to recall include...

Impacted Products
40-lb. cases containing 10-lb. chubs of “Fine Ground Beef.”
