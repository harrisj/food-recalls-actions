Minnesota Firm Recalls Pizza Products Due To Misbranding and Undeclared Allergen

Closed
Fri, 09/27/2013 - Thu, 09/08/2016

WASHINGTON, Sept. 27, 2013 Junction Pizza, a Grey Eagle, Minn. establishment, is recalling approximately 17,194 pounds of frozen pizza products that contain soy lecithin, a known allergen, which is not declared on the product labels, the U.S. Department of Agriculture's Food Safety and Inspection Service...

Impacted Products
30.5-ounce packages of “The Junction Pizza, Italian Sausage Thick Crust” 20-ounce packages of “The Junction Pizza, Italian Sausage Thin Crust”
