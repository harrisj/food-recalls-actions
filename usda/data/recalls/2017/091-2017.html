091-2017 High - Class I Misbranding Unreported Allergens

Kenosha Beef International, LTD. Recalls Beef Products Due To Misbranding and Undeclared Allergens

Closed
Wed, 08/09/2017 - Thu, 10/19/2017
Connecticut, Delaware, Maryland, New Jersey, New York, Pennsylvania

WASHINGTON, Aug. 9, 2017 Kenosha Beef International, LTD., a Kenosha, Wis. establishment, is recalling approximately 3,960 pounds of beef patty products due to misbranding and an undeclared allergen, the U.S. Department of Agricultures Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS) announced today. The products may contain...

Impacted Products
4-lb retail carton containing 12, 1/3 pound burgers of “JP O'REILLY’S, PUB BURGER, FAMILY PACK, MADE FROM BEEF CHUCK,” with a sell-by date of 01-10-18. 24-lb cases of “JP O’REILLY’S, BACON & CHEDDAR BEEF PATTIES” with sell-by date of 01-10-18 and case codes of 0614KS42065, 0614KS42066, 0614KS42067, 0614KS42068 and 0614KS42070.
Quantity Recovered
3,076 pounds
