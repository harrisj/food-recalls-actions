Corn Maiden Foods, Inc. Recalls Ready-to-Eat Beef Products Due To Misbranding

Corn Maiden Foods, Inc.
Closed
Wed, 08/09/2017 - Tue, 12/12/2017
California

WASHINGTON, Aug. 9, 2017 Corn Maiden Foods, Inc., a Harbor City, Calif. establishment, is recalling approximately 2,133 pounds of ready-to-eat (RTE) beef products due to misbranding. The products contain monosodium glutamate (MSG) that was not declared on the product labels, the U.S. Department of Agricultures...

Impacted Products
12-oz packages of “Morrison Aussie Travelers’ Pies, Hand-Held 2 Aussie Pies Steak & Mushroom,” with sell-by dates of Feb. 3, 2018, Feb. 15, 2018, Feb. 16, 2018 and March 29, 2018, and lot codes 170203, 170215, 170216 and 170329.
Quantity Recovered
213 pounds
