Clair D. Thompson & Sons, Inc. Recalls Ground Beef Products Due To Possible E. Coli O157:H7 Contamination

Closed
Wed, 10/04/2017 - Wed, 12/06/2017
New York, Pennsylvania
WASHINGTON, Oct. 4, 2017 Clair D. Thompson & Sons, Inc., a Jersey Shore, Pa. establishment, is recalling approximately 700 pounds of ground beef products that may be contaminated with E. coli O157:H7, the U.S. Department of Agricultures Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS) announced today...
Impacted Products
•5-lb. plastic bags of “Thompson’s GROUND BEEF.” •10-lb. plastic bags of “Thompson’s GROUND BEEF.”
Quantity Recovered
580 pounds
