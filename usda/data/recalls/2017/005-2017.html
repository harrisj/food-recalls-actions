Olymel S.E.C./L.P. Recalls Pork Products Produced Without Benefit of Inspection

Closed
Sat, 01/14/2017 - Fri, 01/27/2017
Michigan

WASHINGTON, Jan. 14, 2017 Olymel S.E.C./ L.P., a Boucherville, Quebec, Canada establishment, is recalling approximately 1,124 pounds of pork ribs products because the products were repacked and distributed without the benefit of federal inspection, the U.S. Department of Agricultures Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS)...

Impacted Products
10-lb. cardboard box of pork rib tips with a packaging date of Jan. 10-13, 2017. 30-lb. cardboard box of pork rib tips with a packaging date of Jan. 10-13, 2017.
Quantity Recovered
6,765 pounds
