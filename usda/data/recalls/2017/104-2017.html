Prestage Foods, Inc. Recalls Turkey Products Due To Possible Extraneous Material Contamination

Closed
Mon, 10/02/2017 - Wed, 02/28/2018
Alabama, Connecticut, Florida, Georgia, New York, North Carolina, Pennsylvania, South Carolina, Tennessee
WASHINGTON, Oct. 2, 2017 Prestage Foods, Inc., a St. Pauls, N.C. establishment, is recalling approximately 38,475 pounds of ground turkey that may be contaminated with extraneous materials, the U.S. Department of Agricultures Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS) announced today.
