P&S Bakery, Inc. Recalls Pepperoni Product Due to Possible Foreign Matter Contamination

P&S Bakery, Inc
Closed
Fri, 06/02/2017 - Thu, 08/24/2017
Ohio, Pennsylvania, Virginia, West Virginia

WASHINGTON, June 2, 2017 P&S Bakery, Inc., a Poland, Ohio establishment, is recalling approximately 2,959 pounds of pepperoni product that may be contaminated with extraneous materials, specifically clear meat casing utilized in food production, the U.S. Department of Agricultures Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS)...

Impacted Products
5.25-oz. clear plastic wrapped packages containing 1 piece of Gia Russa brand “DOUBLE STUFFED Pepperoni Roll PEPPERONI & MOZZARELLA,” with lot/case code 17088.
Quantity Recovered
1,715 pounds
