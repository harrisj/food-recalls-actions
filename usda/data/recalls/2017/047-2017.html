Kabob's Acquisition, Inc. Recalls Meat and Poultry Products Due to Possible Listeria Contamination

Closed

WASHINGTON, May 10, 2017 Kabobs Acquisition, Inc., a Lake City, Ga. establishment, is recalling approximately 5,946 pounds of turkey and ham sandwich products which included a non-meat ingredient that was recalled due to Listeria monocytogenes, the U.S. Department of Agricultures Food Safety and Inspection Service...
