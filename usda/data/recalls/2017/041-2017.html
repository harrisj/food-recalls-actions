Foster Poultry Farms Recalls Frozen Ready-To-Eat Breaded Chicken Patty Products Due to Possible Foreign Matter Contamination

Foster Farms
Closed
Thu, 04/27/2017 - Mon, 06/26/2017
Alaska, Arizona, California, Utah, Washington

WASHINGTON, April 27, 2017 Foster Poultry Farms, a Farmerville, La. establishment, is recalling approximately 131,880 pounds of frozen, ready-to-eat breaded chicken patty products that may be contaminated with foreign materials, specifically plastic, the U.S. Department of Agricultures Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS) announced today...

Impacted Products
5-lb. bags containing 20 pieces of “FOSTER FARMS Chicken Patties BREADED CHICKEN BREAST PATTIES WITH RIB MEAT” with Best By date of 02/15/18.
Quantity Recovered
3,870 pounds
