Nestle Prepared Foods Company Recalls Pork and Beef Products Due To Misbranding and Undeclared Allergens

Closed
Wed, 11/08/2017 - Fri, 12/08/2017
WASHINGTON, Nov. 8, 2017 Nestle Prepared Foods Company, a Mount Sterling, Ky. establishment, is recalling approximately 26,400 pounds of pork and beef products due to misbranding and undeclared allergens, the U.S. Department of Agricultures Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS) announced today. The product(s) contains...
Impacted Products
•2,400-lb. bulk packages of “Nestle Hot Pockets BBQ Burger Bites.” •16,800-lb. bulk packages of “Nestle Hot Pockets 3 Cheese Bacon Bites.” •7,200-lb. bulk packages of “Nestle Hot Pockets Sausage, Egg, and Cheese Snack Bites.”
Quantity Recovered
6,600 pounds
