FSIS Issues Public Health Alert for Summer Sausage Products Due to Possible Foreign Matter Contamination

Swiss Processing Plant Inc.
Active
Sat, 12/18/2021 - Current
Missouri

WASHINGTON, Dec. 18, 2021 – The U.S. Department of Agriculture’s Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS) is issuing a public health alert for approximately 51 pounds of ready-to-eat (RTE) Hawaiian-style summer sausage products due to concerns that the products may be contaminated with extraneous materials...

Impacted Products
8-oz. chub containing RTE Swiss Meat & Sausage Co. HAWAIIAN STYLE SUMMER SAUSAGE with a “MADE ON” date of 111821, located on the left or right edge of the packaging.
