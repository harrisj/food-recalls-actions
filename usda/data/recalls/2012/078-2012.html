New York Firm Recalls Ready-To-Eat Beef and Cheese Pie Products Produced Without Benefit Of Inspection

Closed
Tue, 12/04/2012 - Sat, 06/01/2013
New York

WASHINGTON, Dec 4, 2012 - Cantina Foods, a Buffalo, N.Y. establishment, is recalling approximately 2,375 pounds of ready-to-eat beef and cheese pie products because they were produced without the benefit of federal inspection, the U.S. Department of Agriculture's Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS) announced...

Impacted Products
Cartons containing four clear plastic bags with six 4-oz. "Beef and Cheese Pastellios" in each bag.
Quantity Recovered
58 pounds
