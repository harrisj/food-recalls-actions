North Carolina Firm Recalls Chicken Quesadilla Product Due to Misbranding and an Undeclared Allergen

Smithfield Packaged Meats Corp.
Closed
Wed, 10/03/2012 - Wed, 05/29/2013
Georgia

WASHINGTON, October 5, 2012 - Stefano Foods, Inc., a Charlotte, N.C. establishment, is recalling approximately 3,744 pounds of a chicken quesadilla product because of misbranding and an undeclared allergen. The product contains isolated soy protein, a known allergen, which is not declared on the label...

Impacted Products
18-lb. cases, each containing 12, "QUESADILLA CHICK INS 12/24OZ," with an identifying case code of "F2829" and use by date of "03/31/2013."
Quantity Recovered
1,044 pounds
