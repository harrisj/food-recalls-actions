Texas Firm Recalls Cobb Salads Due To Possible Listeria Contamination

GHSW, LLC
Closed
Fri, 02/03/2012 - Mon, 05/27/2013
Texas

WASHINGTON, Feb. 3, 2012 - GH Foods SW, a Houston, Texas establishment, is recalling approximately 515 pounds of Cobb salad products. The salads contain eggs that are the subject of a Food and Drug Administration (FDA) recall due to concerns about contamination with Listeria monocytogenes...

Impacted Products
7.5-oz. plastic containers of "Fresh Garden Highway Cobb Salad" with a "Sell By" date through 2/6/2012, that bear the establishment number "P-44056" inside the USDA mark of inspection The products were distributed to retail establishments in Texas.
Quantity Recovered
253 pounds
