066-2012 High - Class I Misbranding Produced Without Benefit of Inspection

Texas Firm Recalls Beef and Pork Products Produced Without Benefit of Inspection and Misbranding

Closed
Mon, 10/15/2012 - Wed, 05/29/2013
Louisiana, Texas

WASHINGTON, Oct. 15, 2012 - Lao Chareune Foods, a Dallas, Texas, establishment, is recalling approximately 8,200 pounds of various beef and pork products because they were produced without the benefit of federal inspection and misbranded, the U.S. Department of Agriculture's Food Safety and Inspection Service...

Impacted Products
3-oz. and 8-oz. packages containing "Pork Snack Stick" 3.20-oz. packages containing "Seasoned Fried Beef" 1.76-oz. packages containing "Fried Pork Skins" 1.20-oz. packages containing "Sliced Fried Pork Ears."
Quantity Recovered
183 pounds
