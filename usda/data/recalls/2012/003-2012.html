Michigan Firm Recalls Salad Products for Possible Listeria Contamination

Closed
Fri, 01/27/2012 - Sat, 05/18/2013

WASHINGTON, Jan. 27, 2012 - 18th Street Deli Inc., a Hamtramck, Mich., establishment, is recalling approximately 118 pounds of julienne salad products with turkey, ham and hard-boiled eggs. The salads contain eggs that are the subject of an FDA recall due to contamination with Listeria...

Impacted Products
9.25-ounce packages of "18th Street Deli Julienne Salad," "18th Street Deli Julienne Salad Lite," and "Balanced Choice Julienne Salad Lite" that bear the establishment number "P-22061" inside the USDA mark of inspection and expiration dates of "01/27/12"
Quantity Recovered
29 pounds
