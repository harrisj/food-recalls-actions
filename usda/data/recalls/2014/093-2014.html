Tennessee Firm Recalls Chicken Products Due to Misbranding and Undeclared Allergens

Closed
Wed, 12/31/2015 - Tue, 03/24/2015

WASHINGTON, December 31, 2014 Campos Foods, LLC, a Caryville, Tenn. establishment, is recalling approximately 5,300 pounds of chicken products due to misbranding and undeclared allergens, the U.S. Department of Agriculture's Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS) announced today. Impacted Products
Bulk boxes containing various weights of 90 pieces of “ROCKIN’ ROASTED ROASTED CHICKEN DRUM & THIGH (With Back) Smoke Flavor Added”. 16-lb. bulk boxes containing approximately 32 pieces of “ROCKIN’ ROASTED ROASTED CHICKEN QUARTERS Smoke Flavor Added”.
Quantity Recovered
5,308 pounds
