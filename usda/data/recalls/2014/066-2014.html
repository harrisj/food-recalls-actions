Texas Firm Recalls Ground Beef Product Due To Possible Foreign Matter Contamination

Closed
Sat, 10/04/2014 - Fri, 06/09/2017
Texas

WASHINGTON, Oct. 4, 2014 Sam Kane Beef Processors, a Corpus Christi, Texas establishment, is recalling approximately 90,987 pounds of ground beef products that may be contaminated with extraneous materials, the U.S. Department of Agriculture's FSIS announced today.
