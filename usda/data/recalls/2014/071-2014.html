Maryland Firm Recalls Lamb Products Produced Without Import Inspection

WASHINGTON, OCTOBER 17, 2014 E.&B.'s Natural Way, a Frederick, Md. establishment, is recalling approximately 27,948 pounds of raw lamb products because they were not presented at the border for USDA-FSIS Import Inspection, the U.S. Department of Agriculture's Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS) announced today...
