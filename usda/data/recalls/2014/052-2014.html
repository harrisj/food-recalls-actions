Georgia Firm Recalls Chicken Nugget Product Due To Possible Foreign Matter Contamination

Perdue Foods LLC
Closed
Tue, 08/12/2014 - Tue, 09/23/2014

WASHINGTON, Aug. 12, 2014 Perdue, a Gainesville, Ga. establishment, is recalling approximately 15,306 pounds of frozen, fully cooked chicken nugget product that may be contaminated with extraneous materials, the U.S. Department of Agriculture's FSIS announced today.
