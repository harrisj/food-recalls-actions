Washington Firm Recalls Beef Jerky Products Due to Mislabeling and an Undeclared Allergen

Oberto Snacks Inc.
Closed
Fri, 04/29/2011 - Sat, 06/02/2012
Iowa, Minnesota, North Dakota, Wisconsin

WASHINGTON, April 29, 2011 - Oberto Sausage Company, a Kent, Wash., establishment is recalling approximately 772 pounds of beef jerky products because they contain an undeclared allergen, soy, which is not declared on the label. Soy is a known allergen. The product subject to recall...

Impacted Products
4.16-oz. canisters of "LOWREY'S HICKORY SMOKED BEEF JERKY," containing 3 individual snack packs of 6 beef jerky sticks. These products have a best-by date of "130412 01," printed on the bottom of each canister.
Quantity Recovered
771 pounds
