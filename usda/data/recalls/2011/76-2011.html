Pennsylvania Firm Recalls Pureed Pork Products That May Contain Foreign Materials

Closed

WASHINGTON, Sept 26, 2011 - K. Heeps, Inc., an Allentown, Penn., establishment, is recalling approximately 5,550 pounds of pureed pork products that may contain foreign materials, the U.S. Department of Agriculture's Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS) announced today. The products subject to recall include...

Impacted Products
4.5-lb. boxes of Imperial Sysco "Puree Shaped Meats Country Style Pork and Binder Product"
Quantity Recovered
18 pounds
