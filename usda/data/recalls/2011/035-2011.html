035-2011 Low - Class II Unreported Allergens Mislabeling

Kentucky Firm Recalls Chicken Products Due To Mislabeling and Undeclared Allergen

Closed
Fri, 05/06/2011 - Sat, 06/02/2012
Georgia, Indiana, Kentucky, Ohio, Tennessee

WASHINGTON, May 6, 2011 - Stir the Pot, LLC, a Louisville, Ky. establishment, is recalling approximately 18,648 pounds of chicken products because they contain an undeclared allergen, fish in the form of an anchovy puree in Worcestershire sauce, which is not noted on the label...

Impacted Products
45-lb. cases of "J. Gumbo's Drunken Chicken-Dark Chicken With stewed Tomatoes" with each case containing five, 9-lb. bags with case codes of 2370, 2371, 2372, 2373, 2390, 2391, 2392, 2393, 2394, 2395, 2396, 2374, 2376, 2375, 2377, 2378, 2379, 2386, 2387,
Quantity Recovered
25,313 pounds
