Indiana Firm Recalls Barbecue Beef, Pork and Chicken Products Due To Mislabeling and an Undeclared Allergen

Butterfield Foods, LLC
Closed
Fri, 05/06/2011 - Sat, 06/02/2012
Indiana

WASHINGTON, May 6, 2011 - Butterfield Foods, a Noblesville, Ind., establishment is recalling approximately 285,100 pounds of barbecue beef, pork and chicken products because they contain an undeclared allergen, soy, which is not declared on the label.
