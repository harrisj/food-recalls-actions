Texas Firm Recalls Ready-To-Eat Chicken Products for Possible Listeria Contamination

Pilgrim's Pride Corporation
Closed
Wed, 07/20/2011 - Sat, 06/02/2012
New Jersey, Texas

WASHINGTON, July 20, 2011-– Pilgrim’s Pride Corporation, a firm with establishments in Mount Pleasant, TX and Waco, TX is recalling approximately 11,240 pounds of ready-to-eat chicken products that may be contaminated with Listeria monocytogenes, the U.S. Department of Agriculture’s Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS)...

Impacted Products
10 lb. boxes containing 2 5-lb bags of “Sweet Georgia Brand Fully Cooked Breaded White Chicken Nuggets Shaped Patties” 30 lb. boxes containing 6 5-lb bags of “Pilgrim’s Pride Fully Cooked Grilled Chicken Breast Fillet with Rib Meat”
Quantity Recovered
6,440 pounds
