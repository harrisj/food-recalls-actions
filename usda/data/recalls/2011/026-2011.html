California Firm Recalls Pizza Products Due to Mislabeling and Undeclared Allergen

Closed
Tue, 03/29/2011 - Sat, 06/02/2012

WASHINGTON, March 29, 2011 -Completely Fresh Foods Inc., a Montebello, Calif., establishment is voluntarily recalling approximately 131,000 pounds of pizza products because they contain an allergen, wheat, not declared on the label. Wheat is a known allergen. The products subject to recall include: 10-oz. packages...
