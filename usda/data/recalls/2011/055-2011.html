New York Importer Recalls Bacon Products for Possible Listeria Contamination

Closed
Fri, 07/22/2011 - Sat, 06/02/2012
Florida, Illinois, Indiana, Michigan, Ohio

WASHINGTON, July 22, 2011-– Jetro Cash and Carry Enterprises LLC/R.D. Food Services LP, an importer from New York, is recalling approximately 2,900 pounds of diced bacon products that may be contaminated with Listeria monocytogenes, the U.S. Department of Agriculture’s Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS)...

Impacted Products
10 lb. shipping container with 2, 5 lb. bags boxes containing 2 5-lb bags of “Assoluti Cooked Diced Bacon”
Quantity Recovered
2,300 pounds
