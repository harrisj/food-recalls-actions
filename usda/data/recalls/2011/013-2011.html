013-2011 Low - Class II Mislabeling

Recall Notification Report

J&J Distributing
Closed
Fri, 02/25/2011 - Sat, 06/02/2012
Minnesota

WASHINGTON, Feb. 25, 2011 - J&J Distributing, a St. Paul, Minn., establishment, is recalling approximately 87 pounds of buffalo chicken cheese dip products because they contain monosodium glutamate (MSG) which is not declared on the label. The products subject to recall include: 16-oz. metal containers...

Impacted Products
16-oz. metal containers of "CUB FRESH BUFFALO CHICKEN CHEESE DIP." 16-oz. metal containers of "KOWALSKI'S MARKETS PLACE BUFFALO CHICKEN CHEESE DIP."
Quantity Recovered
24 pounds
