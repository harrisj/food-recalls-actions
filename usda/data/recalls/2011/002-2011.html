002-2011 High - Class I Misbranding Unreported Allergens

California Firm Recalls Tamale Products Due to Mislabeling and an Undeclared Allergen

Closed
Thu, 01/06/2011 - Fri, 06/01/2012
California, Oregon, Washington

WASHINGTON, Jan 6, 2011 - Homestead Pasta Company, a San Francisco, Calif., establishment is recalling approximately 144,633 pounds of frozen meat and poultry tamale products because they contain an undeclared allergen, whey, which is not noted on the label. Whey is a known dairy allergen...

Impacted Products
8.5-lb. cases of "Garibaldi BEEF TAMALE" with each case containing 12 individual 11 oz. packages.
Quantity Recovered
20,012 pounds
