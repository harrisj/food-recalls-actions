Alabama Firm Recalls Frozen Chicken Fillet Products Due to Mislabeling and an Undeclared Allergen

Wayne Farms LLC
Closed
Thu, 03/31/2011 - Sat, 06/02/2012
New Jersey, Massachusetts

WASHINGTON, March 31, 2011 -Wayne Farms, a Decatur, Ala., establishment is recalling approximately 53,210 pounds of frozen, breaded, fully cooked Italian-style chicken breast fillet products because they contain an undeclared allergen, egg, which is not noted on the label.
