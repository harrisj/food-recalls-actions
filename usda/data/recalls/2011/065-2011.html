Minnesota Firm Recalls Summer Sausage Products Due To Misbranding And An Undeclared Alergen

WASHINGTON, August 15, 2011 - Jerry's Foods, an Annandale, Minn. establishment is recalling approximately 24 pounds of summer sausage products because of misbranding and undeclared allergens.
