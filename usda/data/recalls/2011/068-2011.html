California Firm Recalls Chicken Caesar Salad Products Due To Misbranding and Undeclared Allergen

Closed
Fri, 08/26/2011 - Sun, 06/03/2012
Oregon

WASHINGTON, Aug. 26, 2011 - Taylor Farms, a Tracy, Calif. establishment is recalling approximately 52,191 pounds of chicken Caesar salad products because of misbranding and undeclared allergens. The products contain egg, an allergen, which is not noted on the label. The egg is an ingredient...

Impacted Products
7-oz. packages of "Taylor Farms CAESAR PASTA LAYERED SALAD WITH CHICKEN" bearing "P-34013" inside the mark of inspection and a sell by date through Sept. 3, 2011. 12-oz. plastic trays of "Signature Café GRILLED CHICKEN CAESAR SALAD" may or may not bear "P-34013" inside the mark of inspection and bears a sell by date through Sept. 3, 2011. 6.25-lb. cases of "Signature Café GRILLED CHICKEN CAESAR SALAD KIT."
Quantity Recovered
4,193 pounds
