New York Firm Recalls Pork Egg Rolls Due To Misbranding and Undeclared Allergens

Twin Marquis, Inc.
Closed
Wed, 06/22/2011 - Sat, 06/02/2012
New York

WASHINGTON, June 22, 2011 - Chef One, Corp., a Brooklyn, N.Y., establishment is recalling approximately 36 pounds of pork egg rolls because the product is misbranded and contains undeclared allergens. The product was prepared using soy, milk and egg, known allergens which were not declared...

Impacted Products
4 cases of 9 lb. boxes of "Chef One Pork Egg Rolls," containing 50 egg rolls per box.
Quantity Recovered
27 pounds
