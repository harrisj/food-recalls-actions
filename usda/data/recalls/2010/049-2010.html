New York Firm Recalls Deli Meat Products for Possible Listeria Contamination

Closed
Mon, 08/23/2010 - Mon, 06/04/2012

WASHINGTON, August 23, 2010 - Zemco Industries, a Buffalo, N.Y., establishment, is recalling approximately 380,000 pounds of deli meat products that may be contaminated with Listeria monocytogenes, the U.S. Department of Agriculture's Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS) announced today.
