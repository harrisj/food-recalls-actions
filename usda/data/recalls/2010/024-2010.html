024-2010 Low - Class II Unreported Allergens

California Firm Recalls Assorted Meat Products Due To Mislabeling And An Undeclared Allergen

West Lake Food Corporation
Closed
Sat, 04/17/2010 - Sun, 06/03/2012

WASHINGTON, April 17, 2010 - Westlake Food Corporation, a Santa Ana, Calif., establishment, is recalling approximately 140,000 pounds of fully cooked assorted meat products because they contain an undeclared allergen, wheat starch, the U.S. Department of Agriculture's Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS) announced today...

Impacted Products
14-ounce packages of "GIO LUA TAY HO PORK MEAT LOAF WRAPPED IN BANANA LEAVES FULLY COOKED." 13-ounce packages of "BO VIEN TAY HO BEEF MEAT BALL FULLY COOKED."
Quantity Recovered
7,741 pounds
