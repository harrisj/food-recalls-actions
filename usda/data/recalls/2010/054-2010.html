WASHINGTON, Oct. 4, 2010 - Windsor Foods, a Los Angeles, Calif., establishment is recalling approximately 1,608 pounds of Spicy Vegetable Potstickers due to a mislabeling of individual pouches that were packed with Chicken Potstickers. The products subject to recall include: 5-lbs. boxes of 10-ounce cartons...

Quantity Recovered
