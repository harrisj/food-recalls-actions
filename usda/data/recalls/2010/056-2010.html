Wisconsin Firm Recalls Beef Stick Products That May Contain Foreign Materials

Klement's Sausage Company, Inc.
Closed

WASHINGTON, October 22, 2010 - Klement Sausage Company, Inc., a Milwaukee, Wisc., establishment, is recalling approximately 2,740 pounds of beef stick products that may contain foreign materials, the U.S. Department of Agriculture's Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS) announced today.
