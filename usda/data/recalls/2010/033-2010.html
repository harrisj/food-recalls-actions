Illinois Firm Recalls Imported Beef Products Due to Potential Animal Drug Contaminant

WASHINGTON, May 14, 2010- Sampco, Inc., a Chicago, Ill. establishment, is recalling approximately 87,000 pounds of beef products that may contain the animal drug Ivermectin, the U.S. Department of Agriculture's Food Safety and Inspection Service announced today.
