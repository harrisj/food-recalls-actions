New York Firm Recalls Ground Beef Products Due To Possible E. coli O157:H7 Contamination

Crown I Enterprises, Inc.
Closed
Tue, 06/22/2010 - Mon, 06/04/2012
Connecticut, New Jersey, New York

WASHINGTON, June 22, 2010 -Crown I Enterprises, Inc., a Bay Shore, N.Y., establishment, is recalling approximately 3,700 pounds of ground beef products that may be contaminated with E. coli O157:H7, the U.S. Department of Agriculture's Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS) announced today. The products...

Impacted Products
24, 8-ounce burgers in 12-pound boxes of "W.B. STOCKYARD, KEEP REFRIGERATED, BURGER FRESH, WB HOME STYLE 8 OZ." 32, 6-ounce burgers in 12-pound boxes of "W.B. STOCKYARD, KEEP REFRIGERATED, BURGER FRESH 6 OZ."
Quantity Recovered
266 pounds
