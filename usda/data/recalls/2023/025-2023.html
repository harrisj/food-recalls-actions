Distribuidora El Paisano Import, LLC, Recalls Ineligible Ready-To-Eat Pork Rind Product Imported from Guatemala

Active
Tue, 06/20/2023 - Current
Connecticut, Massachusetts, New York, Rhode Island
WASHINGTON, June 20, 2023 - Distribuidora El Paisano Import, LLC, a distributor and the importer of record located in Providence, R.I., is recalling approximately 1,715 pounds of imported ready-to-eat pork rind product that was imported from Guatemala, a country ineligible to export meat products to...
Impacted Products
5.3 oz. (150g) foil pouch packages containing "TorTrix Con Chicharrón."
