Daniele International LLC Recalls Ready-to-Eat Sausage Products Due to Possible Listeria Contamination

Daniele International
Active
Sun, 01/29/2023 - Current

WASHINGTON, Jan. 29, 2023 – Daniele International LLC, a Mapleville, R.I., establishment, is recalling approximately 52,914 pounds of ready-to-eat (RTE) sausage products that may be adulterated with Listeria monocytogenes, the U.S. Department of Agriculture’s Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS) announced today.
