Custom Made Meals, LLC Recalls Bacon Wrapped Chicken Products Due to Misbranding and Undeclared Allergen

Custom Made Meals
Active
Sat, 09/09/2023 - Current

WASHINGTON, Sep. 09, 2023 – Custom Made Meals, LLC, a Denver, Col. establishment, is recalling approximately 1,345 pounds of products labeled as Bacon-Wrapped BBQ Seasoned Chicken Grillers, but contain Bacon-Wrapped Jalapeno Peppers Stuffed with Cream Cheese due to misbranding and undeclared allergen, the U.S. Department...

Impacted Products
Variable weight black plastic trays with clear top film labeled as “Bacon-Wrapped BBQ Seasoned Chicken Grillers” and a code date of “USE BY 21-SEPT-23” represented on the label.
