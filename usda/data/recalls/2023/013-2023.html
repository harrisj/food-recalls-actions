Bruno Beef Strips Recalls Beef Strip Products Due to Misbranding and an Undeclared Allergen

Active
Thu, 04/20/2023 - Current
Illinois, Iowa, Wisconsin

WASHINGTON, April 20, 2023 – Bruno Beef Strips, a Monroe, Wis. establishment, is recalling approximately 7,452 pounds of ready-to-eat (RTE) snack stick beef strips due to misbranding and an undeclared allergen, the U.S. Department of Agriculture’s Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS) announced today.
