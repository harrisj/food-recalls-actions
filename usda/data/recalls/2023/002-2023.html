Wild Cajun Meals LLC Recalls Frozen, Fully Cooked Jambalaya and Gumbo Products Produced without Benefit of Inspection

Active
Mon, 01/30/2023 - Current
Arkansas, Louisiana, Texas
WASHINGTON, Jan. 30, 2023 – Wild Cajun Meals LLC, a Garland, Texas establishment, is recalling approximately 18,418 pounds of frozen, fully cooked jambalaya and gumbo products that were produced without the benefit of federal inspection, the U.S. Department of Agriculture’s Food Safety and Inspection Service...
Impacted Products
32-oz. Plastic containers of “Lady Jambalaya Homemade Jambalaya.” 32-oz. Plastic containers of “Lady Jambalaya Homemade Gumbo.”
