JYC Enterprise, Inc. Recalls Chicken and Pork Potsticker Products Produced Without Benefit of Inspection

JYC Enterprise, Inc.
Active
Fri, 10/28/2022 - Current
Wisconsin

WASHINGTON, Oct. 28, 2022 – JYC Enterprise, Inc., a Houston, Texas establishment, is recalling approximately 33,280 pounds of frozen chicken and pork potsticker products that were repackaged, relabeled, and distributed without the benefit of federal inspection, the U.S. Department of Agriculture’s Food Safety and Inspection...

Impacted Products
• 16-lb. cases of “ASIAN PRIDE POTSTICKERS” containing 330 pieces of “CHICKEN POTSTICKER” in 8 2-lb. clear plastic bags. • 16-lb. cases of “ASIAN PRIDE POTSTICKERS” containing 330 pieces of “PORK POTSTICKER” in 8 2-lb. clear plastic bags.
