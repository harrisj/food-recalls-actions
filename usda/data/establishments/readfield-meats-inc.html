Readfield Meats, Inc. Recalls Beef Trimming Products Due to Possible Adulteration

WASHINGTON, May 27, 2017 Readfield Meats, Inc., doing business as Ruffino Meats & Food Service, a Bryan, Texas establishment, is recalling approximately 45,985 pounds of beef trimming products due to possible adulteration, the U.S. Department of Agricultures Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS) announced today...

Impacted Products
50-lb. boxes containing “BEEF TRIMMINGS FOR COOKING ONLY.”
Quantity Recovered
