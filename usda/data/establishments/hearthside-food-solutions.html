Hearthside Food Solutions, LLC Recalls Ready-To-Eat Sausage and Bacon Breakfast Sandwiches Due to Misbranding and an Undeclared Allergen

Hearthside Food Solutions
Active

WASHINGTON, April 2, 2025 – Hearthside Food Solutions, LLC, a Salt Lake City, Utah establishment, is recalling approximately 489,887 pounds of ready-to-eat (RTE) sausage and bacon breakfast sandwich products due to misbranding and an undeclared allergen, the U.S. Department of Agriculture’s Food Safety and Inspection...

Impacted Products
8.35-oz. individually film wrapped sandwiches of “LETTIERI’S FOOD TO GO Sausage, Egg & Cheese FRENCH TOAST BREAKFAST SANDWICH” with lot codes beginning with the numbers 23, 24 and 25 up to 2508451. 7.3-oz. individually film wrapped sandwiches of “LETTIERI’S FOOD TO GO Bacon, Egg & Cheese FRENCH TOAST BREAKFAST SANDWICH” with lot codes beginning with the numbers 23, 24 and 25 up to 2508451.
